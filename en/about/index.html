Who?


I'm Yusuf Aristil, a senior high-school student from Finland. I focus on documenting the stunning world of aviation and it's different industries. Growing up next to the largest airport in Finland, I've naturally grown a strong passion for aviation, especially for the technical aspects of it. Call me a tech savvy if you will.

For the past 5 years, I've been documenting aviation action at Helsinki Airport, among other places. I've published my quality photos and videos online (My portfolio website, Instagram, Airplanepictures.net), and received praise for my work among airlines and companies in the aviation industry.

This project, Airframe, is my way to help others showcase their world of aviation through memorable visuals.
