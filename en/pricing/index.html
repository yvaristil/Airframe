Pricing structure


Each project starts with a free project briefing, during which we go over the specific details of the project, such as the shooting plan, any notices, and the final quote. The briefing can be done via email, video call, or in whatever way is most convenient for you.

On-site photography and videography, depending on the equipment and complexity starting at:
29 € / hour

For photo and video post-processing, depending on the complexity starting at:
19 € / hour 

For travel expenses:
15 € per hour of travel to the shooting location.

Other material expenses go under 40 €.

Examples:
A photo shoot, lasting 1 hour, with post-processing for 15 images is approximately 
60 € + travel & material expenses.

A video shoot, lasting 1 hour, with post-processing for a 15 min video is approximately
90 € + travel & material expenses.


Further information:

• After the shoot, you will receive a selection of photos and/or videos from which the final materials for post-processing will be chosen. The photos and videos will be delivered as agreed.

• Additional photos beyond the previously agreed-upon number can be purchased for 3 € per photo. Additional edits for videos are charged at a rate of 10 € per hour.
