Videography

I've been making videos ever since I created my first silly stop-motion animations with my phone when I was 10 years old. Over the years I've helped create videos for diverse projects, such as Erasmus+ programs, recaps for events with hundreds of attendees, and my more.
Many of my learnings from aviation photography translate straight to my video work. These days use my Canon equipment for subjects that are further away, my Samsung A26 for close-up shots and a 4K action camera for first-person videos. 

This service could suite you, if:

• You are looking to sell or market your aircraft, and require high-quality videos.
• You are looking to document your event at an airport (airshows, new route commencements).
• You have another project in mind that requires a professional aviation videography.
